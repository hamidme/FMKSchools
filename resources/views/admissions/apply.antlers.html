How to Apply

Joining the FMKS family is straightforward. Follow the steps below and we will guide you every step of the way.

{{ partial:partials/admissions-subnav }} {{# Application steps #}}
The Process

Four Simple Steps

1

Enquire

Call us, send an email, or walk into our admissions office. Our friendly team will answer all your questions and send you an information pack.

2

Visit

Book a school tour or attend one of our Open Days. See our facilities, meet our teachers, and get a feel for the FMKS community.

3

Apply

Complete and submit the application form together with the required documents and the non-refundable application fee.

4

Enrol

Upon acceptance, pay the acceptance deposit, complete the enrolment forms, and your child officially becomes part of the FMKS family.
