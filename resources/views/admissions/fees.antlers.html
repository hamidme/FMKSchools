{{# Scholarships #}}
Financial Support

Scholarships &
Bursaries

We believe that financial constraints should not prevent a talented child from accessing an FMKS education. We offer a number of merit and needs-based support options each session.

All scholarship applications are assessed on a case-by-case basis by our admissions panel. Applicants must meet the academic and character criteria set by the school.

Academic Excellence Award

Awarded to incoming JSS 1 and SSS 1 students with outstanding primary and junior secondary results respectively. Covers up to 50% of tuition fees.

Needs-Based Bursary

For families facing genuine financial hardship. Applicants must provide supporting documentation. Reviewed annually.

Sibling Discount

Families with two or more children enrolled at FMKS receive a 10% discount on fees for the third child and beyond.
