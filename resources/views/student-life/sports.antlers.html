Our Philosophy

Every Student Is
an Athlete

Physical Education is compulsory at all levels in FMKS. We believe that sport builds not just physical strength, but the mental toughness, discipline, and team spirit that define great character.

Our inter-house sports system creates healthy competition within the school, while our representative teams compete in inter-school leagues and state-level competitions. Students who show exceptional talent receive additional coaching and exposure.
