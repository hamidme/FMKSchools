Why It Matters

School Is More Than
Academics

At FMKS, we believe that a truly great education happens both inside and outside the classroom. Our clubs and societies are designed to help students explore their interests, discover hidden talents, and develop the soft skills — leadership, teamwork, public speaking — that will shape their futures.

Every student from Primary 3 upwards is encouraged to join at least one club. Activities run every Friday afternoon and are led by teachers who are genuinely passionate about the subjects they oversee.

Our Clubs & Societies

Debate Society

JSS – SSS

Students argue topical motions, build critical thinking, and represent FMKS in inter-school debate competitions across Lagos.

Science Club

Primary – SSS

Weekly experiments, STEM challenges, and science fair preparation. Past members have won national science Olympiad medals.

Drama Society

Primary – SSS

Stage productions, cultural performances, and end-of-year shows. Builds confidence, expression, and public presence.

Music Club

All Arms

Choir, keyboard, and percussion. Students perform at school events, Open Days, and community outreach programmes.

Literary & Press Club

JSS – SSS

Creative writing, poetry, and the school magazine. Members develop a voice and learn the craft of storytelling and journalism.

Coding & Tech Club

JSS – SSS

HTML, Python, robotics basics, and app design. Students have won state-level tech competitions and built projects for real community needs.
