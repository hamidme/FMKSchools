Strong Foundations,
Bright Futures

Our Primary School programme spans six years (Primary 1–6) and is designed to give every child a solid grounding in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking — the three pillars on which all future academic success is built.

Teaching is interactive and inquiry-based. We move away from rote learning toward understanding, application, and discovery. Our teachers guide children to ask questions, solve problems, and express themselves clearly and confidently.

By Primary 6, our students are fully prepared for the Common Entrance examination and the demands of a rigorous secondary education.

{{# Key milestones #}}
Progress

Key Milestones

Pry 1–2

Foundation Stage

Solidifying reading, handwriting, and number fluency. Building classroom habits and a love for learning through short, engaging lessons.

Pry 3–4

Development Stage

Deepening subject knowledge, introducing essay writing, fractions, and project-based learning. Growing independence and critical thinking.

Pry 5–6

Excellence Stage

Intensive Common Entrance preparation, mock exams, and FMKS's own internal promotion assessments to ensure readiness for JSS 1.
