Our Approach

Learning Through Play,
Growing Through Love

At FMKS, we believe the early years are the most important years of a child's life. Our Early Years programme is built on a play-based, child-centred philosophy that balances structured learning with free exploration.

Every classroom is a safe, warm, and stimulating environment where children develop language, social skills, early numeracy, and a lifelong love of learning — at their own pace, supported by trained and caring teachers.

We follow a curriculum aligned with Nigeria's Early Childhood Education standards, enriched with phonics, creative arts, music, and physical development activities.

Age Groups

Classes in Our Early Years

Crèche

3 months – 2 years

Supervised care in a warm, home-like environment. Feeding schedules, sensory play, and infant development activities.

Nursery 1 & 2

2 – 4 years

Early language development, counting, colours, shapes, and social skills through structured play and storytelling.

Kindergarten 1

4 – 5 years

Introduction to phonics and letter formation, number work to 20, creative arts, and group activities that build confidence.

Kindergarten 2

5 – 6 years

Full reading readiness, writing sentences, number operations, and smooth transition preparation into Primary 1.

Our Early Years curriculum covers all areas of a young child's development — cognitive, language, social, emotional, and physical. No single area is neglected.

Children enjoy structured lessons in the mornings and discovery-based play in the afternoons, creating a rhythm that is both stimulating and reassuring.
