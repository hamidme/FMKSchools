Message from our Principal

Welcome to Future Model of Knowledge Schools

I believe that every child carries within them an extraordinary potential — waiting to be discovered, nurtured, and set free. At FMKS, we are committed to creating an environment where that potential is not just recognised, but celebrated.

Our school is built on the conviction that true education goes beyond examinations. It shapes character, builds resilience, and lights the path toward a purposeful life. We partner with every family to ensure your child is known, valued, and challenged to grow.

Knowledge, as our motto declares, is Light and Power. It is the most enduring gift we can give a child — one that no circumstance can diminish or take away. We believe in equipping your child with quality education and are committed to pursuing excellence at every stage of their journey.

From our Early Years classrooms filled with curiosity and colour, to our Senior Secondary students preparing confidently for WAEC and beyond — every day at FMKS is a purposeful step forward. Our teachers are not merely instructors; they are mentors, champions, and constant encouragers of every student in their care.

I invite you to visit our school, meet our community, and experience firsthand what makes FMKS a place where children truly flourish. We look forward to welcoming your family into ours.

The Principal

Future Model of Knowledge Schools
