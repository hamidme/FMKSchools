The Beginning

Two Decades of Lighting
the Path to Knowledge

Founded in 2005, Future Model of Knowledge Schools began with a simple but powerful conviction: that every child in Nigeria deserves access to world-class education rooted in strong values.

What started as a small nursery and primary school has grown into a full-service institution serving students from Crèche through Senior Secondary — building a legacy of academic excellence, moral integrity, and holistic development along the way.

Our motto, "Knowledge is Light & Power," is not just words on a wall. It guides every decision we make — from the teachers we hire, to the curriculum we design, to the way we treat every child who walks through our gates.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional and inclusive education that equips every student with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be Nigeria's foremost model school — recognised for producing graduates of academic excellence, strong character, and genuine compassion.

Our Values

Excellence · Integrity · Respect · Faith · Innovation · Service. These are not aspirations — they are the daily standard we hold ourselves to.
