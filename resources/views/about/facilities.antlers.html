Our Campus

A Campus Built for Learning

Modern, safe, and purposefully designed — every space at FMKS supports curiosity, collaboration, and growth.

Modern Classrooms

Bright, well-ventilated classrooms equipped with projectors, whiteboards, and learning materials for every subject and level.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Biology, Chemistry, and Physics labs where students conduct hands-on experiments that bring theory to life.

Library & Resource Centre

A well-stocked library with thousands of titles — fiction, non-fiction, and academic references — open daily to all students.

ICT Centre

A dedicated computer lab with high-speed internet, teaching students digital literacy, coding fundamentals, and productivity tools.

Sports & Recreation

Football pitch, basketball court, and outdoor recreation areas that keep students active, healthy, and team-spirited.

Assembly Hall

A large multipurpose hall used for morning assemblies, debates, cultural events, prize-giving days, and school productions.
