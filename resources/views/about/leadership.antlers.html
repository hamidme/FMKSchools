Principal

The Principal

A seasoned educator with over 20 years of experience in Nigerian private schooling, our Principal has dedicated her career to the belief that every child can achieve greatness when given the right environment and guidance.

Under her leadership, FMKS has recorded its highest-ever WAEC results, expanded to a full Senior Secondary, and built a culture of excellence that runs through every classroom and corridor.

She holds a Masters in Education Administration and is an active member of the Nigerian Association of Proprietors of Schools (NAPS).
