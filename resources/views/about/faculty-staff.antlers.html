Teachers Who Go Beyond

Every FMKS teacher is recruited not just for academic qualification, but for passion, patience, and a genuine love for children. We believe that great teaching transforms lives — and we hire accordingly.

Our staff participate in continuous professional development programmes, both in-house and external, to stay current with best practices in education and child development.

We maintain a healthy student-to-teacher ratio across all classes, ensuring every child receives the individual attention they deserve to reach their full potential.
